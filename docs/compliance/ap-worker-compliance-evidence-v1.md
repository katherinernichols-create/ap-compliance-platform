# Associated Provider Worker Compliance Evidence Requirements

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 30 November 2025  
**Status:** Draft for Prototype  
**Purpose:** Minimum evidence requirements for AP workers to demonstrate compliance to Registered Providers

---

## Executive Summary

This document defines the minimum evidence Associated Providers (APs) must maintain and provide to Registered Providers (RPs) to demonstrate worker compliance under the Aged Care Act 2024 and Strengthened Aged Care Quality Standards (February 2025).

**Key Principle:** Under Quality Standard 2.9.1, providers must "maintain records of aged care worker pre-employment checks, contact details, qualifications and experience."

---

## 1. MANDATORY SCREENING & CLEARANCES

### 1.1 Police Certificate OR NDIS Clearance Decision

**Workers must provide ONE of the following:**

#### Option A: NDIS Clearance Decision (Preferred)
- **Validity Period:** 5 years maximum
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Current NDIS Worker Screening Clearance certificate
  - Clearance number
  - Issue and expiry dates
  - Worker's full name and date of birth
  - Record must show clearance is "in force"
- **Renewal Trigger:** 90 days before expiry
- **Regulatory Basis:** Satisfies worker screening requirements under the Rules

#### Option B: National Police Check
- **Validity Period:** 3 years maximum
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Copy of Police Certificate (National Police Check)
  - Issue date clearly visible (must be within 3 years)
  - Worker's full name matching employment records
  - Certificate must confirm person has **NOT** been convicted of:
    - **Murder**
    - **Sexual assault**
    - Any other form of **assault** for which they were **sentenced to imprisonment**
- **Renewal Trigger:** 90 days before expiry
- **Regulatory Basis:** Alternative to NDIS clearance; Quality Standard 2.9.1

**Critical Note:** If Police Check shows convictions for murder, sexual assault, or imprisonment for assault, worker is **automatically ineligible** for aged care work.

### 1.2 International Criminal History Declaration
**Required if:** Worker has been a citizen or permanent resident of any country other than Australia after turning 16 years old.

- **Evidence Required:**
  - **Statutory Declaration** stating the person has **never** been convicted in any other country of:
    - Murder
    - Sexual assault  
    - Any other form of assault for which they were sentenced to imprisonment
  - Declaration must be signed and witnessed
  - Must cover ALL countries where person has been citizen/permanent resident since age 16
- **Regulatory Basis:** Worker screening requirements under the Rules
- **Storage:** RP must retain copy of statutory declaration

### 1.3 State/Territory-Specific Screening (If Applicable)

#### Working with Children Check
**Required if:** Worker delivers services to persons under 18 years old
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Current Working with Children Check card/certificate
  - Card number and expiry date
  - Varies by state/territory (e.g., WWCC in NSW, WWC Check in VIC)
- **Validity:** Typically 5 years (varies by state)

#### Working with Vulnerable People (TAS/ACT)
**Required if:** Worker operates in Tasmania or Australian Capital Territory
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Working with Vulnerable People registration card
  - Registration number
  - Expiry date
- **Validity:** Varies by jurisdiction

**Note:** State/territory requirements are IN ADDITION to Police Check or NDIS clearance, not a replacement.

## 2. QUALIFICATIONS & CERTIFICATIONS

### 2.1 Role-Specific Qualifications

#### Personal Care Assistants (PCAs)
- **Certificate III in Individual Support (Ageing)** OR equivalent
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Official qualification certificate
  - Institution name and accreditation details
  - Date of completion
  - Transcript (if requested by RP)

#### Registered Nurses (RNs)
- **AHPRA Registration (Nursing and Midwifery Board)**
- **Validity Period:** Annual renewal
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Current AHPRA registration certificate
  - Registration number
  - Renewal date
  - Any conditions, endorsements, or undertakings
  - Proof of professional indemnity insurance arrangements
- **Verification:** RPs can independently verify via AHPRA public register (www.ahpra.gov.au)
- **Critical:** Registration must be current and unrestricted

#### Other Regulated Professions (Allied Health)
If worker provides physiotherapy, occupational therapy, dietetics, podiatry, etc.:
- **Current professional registration** under relevant National Law or certification body
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Registration certificate from relevant board (e.g., Physiotherapy Board, Occupational Therapy Board)
  - Registration number
  - Expiry/renewal date
  - Scope of practice limitations (if any)

### 2.3 Agency/Subcontractor Agreement Documentation
**Required:** Associated Provider's agreement with Registered Provider must include:
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Copy of signed AP-RP agreement/contract
  - Schedule detailing which party is responsible for:
    - Verifying worker qualifications
    - Maintaining worker training records
    - Conducting police checks/screening
    - Performance monitoring
  - Indemnity clauses and insurance coverage
- **Purpose:** Clarifies compliance responsibilities between AP and RP

## 3. MANDATORY TRAINING RECORDS

Based on Quality Standard 2.9.6 requirements for "competency-based training in relation to core matters":

### 3.1 CPR Certification (Current)
- **Validity Period:** 12 months
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Certificate from accredited provider (e.g., Australian Resuscitation Council)
  - Issue date and expiry date
  - Course code (HLTAID009 or current equivalent)
- **Renewal Trigger:** 30 days before expiry (critical safety requirement)

### 3.2 Manual Handling Training
- **Validity Period:** 12 months (annual refresher)
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Certificate of completion
  - Date of training
  - Training provider details
- **Standard Reference:** Quality Standard 2.9.6 (competency-based training)

### 3.3 Infection Control Training
- **Validity Period:** 12 months (annual update)
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Certificate or training record
  - Completion date
  - Training content confirmation (must cover aged care context)
- **Standard Reference:** Quality Standard 2.9.6

### 3.4 Core Competency Training (Quality Standard 2.9.6)

Workers must have evidence of training in the following areas (refreshed regularly):

a) **Person-centred, rights-based care**
   - Evidence: Training certificate or completion record
   - Frequency: Induction + annual refresher

b) **Culturally safe, trauma-aware care**
   - Evidence: Training certificate
   - Frequency: Induction + biennial refresher

c) **Dementia care**
   - Evidence: Completion certificate
   - Frequency: Induction + annual refresher

d) **Medical emergency response**
   - Evidence: Training record
   - Frequency: Annual

e) **Code of Conduct, SIRS, Quality Standards**
   - Evidence: Acknowledgment and training completion
   - Frequency: Induction + annual update

## 4. EMPLOYMENT & IDENTITY VERIFICATION

### 4.1 Pre-Employment Checks Record
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Proof of identity (100-point ID check record)
  - Work rights verification (for non-citizens)
  - Employment history verification (minimum 2 referees)
  - Professional references

### 4.2 Contact Details
- Current address
- Phone number
- Emergency contact
- **Regulatory Basis:** Quality Standard 2.9.1

---

## 5. ONGOING PERFORMANCE & SUPERVISION

### 5.1 Performance Reviews
- **Evidence:**
  - Annual performance assessment records
  - Supervision meeting notes (minimum quarterly)
- **Regulatory Basis:** Quality Standard 2.9.7 ("regular assessment, monitoring and review of performance")

### 5.2 Incident & Complaint Records
- **Evidence:**
  - Any SIRS notifications involving the worker
  - Complaint records and resolutions
  - Corrective action plans (if applicable)
- **Purpose:** Demonstrates ongoing suitability and professional conduct

## 6. ASSOCIATED PROVIDER ENTITY REQUIREMENTS

These are **organisation-level** requirements that validate the AP itself is compliant (not individual worker credentials, but necessary for RP due diligence):

### 6.1 Professional Indemnity Insurance
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Current Certificate of Currency
  - Coverage amount (minimum as specified in AP-RP agreement)
  - Policy number and expiry date
  - Insurer details
- **Minimum Coverage:** Typically $20 million (varies by agreement)
- **Renewal Trigger:** 60 days before expiry

### 6.2 Public Liability Insurance  
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Current Certificate of Currency
  - Coverage amount (minimum as specified in AP-RP agreement)
  - Policy number and expiry date
  - Insurer details
- **Minimum Coverage:** Typically $20 million (varies by agreement)
- **Renewal Trigger:** 60 days before expiry

### 6.3 Workers Compensation Insurance
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Current Certificate of Currency (as required by state/territory)
  - Employer details and policy number
- **Regulatory Requirement:** Mandatory under state/territory workers compensation legislation

### 6.4 Business Registration & Compliance
- **Evidence Required:**
  - ABN/ACN verification
  - Business registration certificates
  - Relevant industry accreditations (e.g., quality certifications)
- **Purpose:** Validates AP's legitimacy as a service provider

**Note:** These documents are typically uploaded once by the AP and shared across multiple RPs in a "compliance passport" model, supporting "Single Submission, Multiple Uses" efficiency.

## 7. OPTIONAL/ROLE-SPECIFIC EVIDENCE

### 7.1 First Aid Certificate
- For workers in supervisory roles or working in home care
- **Validity:** Typically 3 years
- **Evidence:** Current First Aid certificate (HLTAID011 or equivalent)

### 7.2 Medication Administration
- For workers authorised to administer medications
- **Evidence:**
  - Medication competency certificate
  - Signed delegation document (from RN)
  - Annual competency assessment

### 7.3 Specialist Training
- Palliative care
- Complex wound care
- Diabetes management
- **Evidence:** Certificates from recognised training providers

---

## 8. EVIDENCE STORAGE & FORMAT REQUIREMENTS

### 8.1 Document Standards
- All evidence must be:
  - **Legible**: Clear scans or photos
  - **Complete**: No partial documents
  - **Verifiable**: Include provider/issuer details
  - **Current**: Within validity period (or renewal in progress)

### 8.2 Digital Requirements for Kora Platform
- **Accepted Formats:** PDF, JPG, PNG
- **Maximum File Size:** 5MB per document
- **Naming Convention:** `WorkerName_CredentialType_IssueDate.pdf`
- **Security:** Encrypted storage, access logged

### 8.3 Data Retention
- **Minimum:** 7 years from worker's last engagement (as per regulatory requirements)
- **Privacy:** Must comply with Privacy Act 1988

## 9. COMPLIANCE VERIFICATION WORKFLOW

### For Associated Providers (APs):
1. **Onboarding:** Collect all mandatory evidence before worker's first shift
2. **Verification:** Verify authenticity of certificates and qualifications
3. **Upload:** Store securely in Kora platform
4. **Monitor:** Track expiry dates with automated alerts (90, 60, 30 days)
5. **Renewal:** Ensure updates uploaded before expiry
6. **Audit-Ready:** Maintain complete, current records for RP review

### For Registered Providers (RPs):
1. **Request Access:** View AP worker compliance dashboard via Kora
2. **Review Evidence:** Check all mandatory requirements met
3. **Verify Currency:** Confirm no expired credentials
4. **Flag Issues:** Request updates for missing/expiring evidence
5. **Approve:** Confirm worker compliant for service delivery
6. **Ongoing:** Monitor compliance status in real-time
